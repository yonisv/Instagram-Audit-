# Who's Actually Winning on Instagram?
### An Independent Instagram Strategy Audit — Yonis Velasquez · 2026

---

## Overview

Six brands. Six different Instagram strategies. One honest verdict on what's working and why.

This is a judgment-based brand audit — not a data dump. I went through the Instagram pages of six real brands, analyzed what they're doing, graded them on a 10-point scale across five criteria, and wrote 4–5 paragraphs of real analysis in my own voice. Every grade is defensible. Every observation is mine.

## Scoring Criteria

Each brand was graded across five dimensions:

- **Content Quality** — Is the content itself worth consuming?
- **Audience Understanding** — Does the brand know exactly who it's talking to?
- **Brand Consistency** — Does the feed feel cohesive and intentional?
- **Community Engagement** — Is the brand in the conversation or just broadcasting?
- **Strategic Vision** — Is there a clear, long-term playbook behind the account?

---

## The Core Thesis

> *"The best Instagram strategies aren't about follower count. They're about understanding exactly who you're talking to — and refusing to talk to anyone else."*

Red Bull has 32.7M followers. Costa Sunglasses has 565K. Costa earns a 7. The difference isn't resources — it's self-awareness.
